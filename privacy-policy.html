Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Old English Translator is designed as a simple browser-based tool. This policy explains what information may be stored or processed when you use the website.

Information you enter

The translator lets you type or paste text for conversion. The offline translation rules run in your browser. If the site is used only as a static page, your text does not need to leave your device for translation.

Local storage

The site may use browser local storage to save your theme preference and recent translation history. This information stays in your browser on your device. You can clear translation history inside the tool or clear site data in your browser settings.

Contact page

The contact form opens your email app using a mailto link. Your name, email, and message are placed into an email draft on your device. The message is sent only if you choose to send it from your email client.

Cookies and accounts

The static tool does not require an account. The core translator does not need cookies to function. If future hosting adds analytics, advertising, or server features, the hosted site should disclose those services here.

Children's privacy

The site is intended as a general language and writing tool. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Users should avoid entering private personal details into any translation tool.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact contact@translatetooldenglish.com.
